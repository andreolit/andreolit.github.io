An initiative for first-year engineering students through the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering. STEPUP provides an accelerated introduction to university life through academic enrichment, hands-on projects, and professional development. The program fosters a strong support network of mentors, faculty, and industry leaders, combining academic rigor with team-building experiences to build confidence and set the foundation for success in engineering.
